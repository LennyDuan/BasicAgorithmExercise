You have some small balls which were marked with number 1, 2, 3, ...., n from left to right.
You have two movement option. First is AXY mean move X to the left of Y ball.
Second is BXY means you can move Ball X to the right of Y.
For exampe, if you carry out an order A 1 4. The ball 1 will move to the left of 4.
Input the number of Balls N, number of orders and operation. Out put the final result.

Sample Input : 6 2 
               A 1 4
               B 3 5
Sample Output: 2 1 4 5 3 6
